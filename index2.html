Square

Cube
Area = 6a² Volume = a³
Rectangle
Perimeter = 2(l + w) Area = l × w
Rectangular Prism
Surface Area = 2(lw + lh + wh) Volume = l × w × h
Triangle
Perimeter ≈ 2b Area = (b × h) / 2
Triangular Pyramid
Surface Area ≈ b × h + b² Volume = (b² × H) / 3
Circle
Perimeter = 2πr Area = πr²
Sphere
Surface Area = 4πr² Volume = (4/3)πr³
Cylinder
Surface Area = 2πr² + 2πrh Volume = πr²h
Cone
Surface Area ≈ πr(r + √(r² + h²)) Volume = (1/3)πr²h
